Welcome to the Rogue Lite Typing Game - RLTG

Welcome the to the Typing Rogue Lite Game, the goal is simple, reach the end of each dungeon and gain as much gold as you can!

How do you earn gold you ask? Well, you can reach the end of the dungeon and get a chest which contains gold, but to get there you will need to defeat monsters, these monsters dont go down by a sword or magic staff though... They must be killed with words!
